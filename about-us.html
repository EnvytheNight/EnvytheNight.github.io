We do more than just offer advice; we proffer and deliver solutions as we turn individual professional knowledge and skill into team work.

Our strengths lie in the quality of the services we provide, the extensive experience and knowledge of our people, attention to detail and timely delivery of fit for purpose and value driven business solutions for our clients. This is even made better by the incorporation of our corporate consulting associates. Our people learn the business of our clients, take time to listen and work efficiently and proactively to offer tailored solutions geared at promoting business process efficiency and maximising returns on investment.

We render audit and consultancy services to both private and public sectors by using internationally accepted auditing procedures and guidelines in compliance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) as well as other relevant statutory provisions such as CBN guidelines, NDIC (Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation) etc.

In conjunction with our management and re-branding corporate consultant, we carry out the installation of Human Resource departments for new and up-coming establishments and re-organization of existing human resource departments by applying relevant labour laws to meet international human resource management standards as well as re-branding of our client companies to harmonize employee and company interests through the institutionalizing of best corporate governance and standard of behaviour that is globally acceptable. We advise on communal crisis management and represent organizations at staff union meetings and remuneration negotiations (if this must be part of our clients’ survival instincts, otherwise, we work to discourage unionism where it would be to best interest of our client). Under this service, we carry out severance benefit calculation and harmonize employee-employer relationship.
